Q2. RESPONSE OF SYSTEM a=0.5

a=0.5;
b=4.5;
c=[1,-a];
n=0:100;
h = impz(b,c,n);
x=3*sin(2*pi*0.2*n); %input signal
y=conv(h,x);
figure()
stem(y)
xlabel('time')
ylabel('Amplitude')
title('response a=0.5')
xlim([0 100])
